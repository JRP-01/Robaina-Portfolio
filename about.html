About Me

Hello, my name is Jose Robaina Perez. I am a Cuban-American, born in Cuba, and I come from a proud family of tobacco producers. Growing up in this environment instilled in me a strong respect for tradition, discipline, and resilience—qualities that I now apply to my personal and professional growth.

I currently work in Amazon’s Supply Chain, where I focus on efficiency, accuracy, and problem-solving across nationwide operations. My role has allowed me to develop strong organizational and analytical skills while also contributing to process improvements that save time and reduce errors.

Alongside my professional role, I am pursuing a degree in Computer Programming. My goal is to combine my technical expertise, creativity, and problem-solving skills to build meaningful solutions in the technology field. This portfolio reflects my progress and serves as a platform to showcase the projects I develop throughout my academic and professional journey.
